About Us

Greetings!! Thank you for checking out our site. We ask, is it possible to be a steward of these beautiful islands, while promoting businesses? Can we enjoy these amazingly beautiful islands and be more mindful of our consumption and waste? Which are the community members and local businesses that ask this same question? And which ones answer with a resounding, "YES!"? We aim to meet and support anyone in the community who is already doing this in their own way. We feel that our islands and their resources should be treated as a COMMUNITY and never just a COMMODITY. We are motivated by a love for the hospitality industry, sustainability, and Caribbean culture, and have built this site to support local Virgin Island businesses and creatives who embrace togetherness, and inspire sustainable practices. All of this as we share information on current events and happenings of interest for visitors and locals alike. Welcome to our islands. Enjoy!!!
