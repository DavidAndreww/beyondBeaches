Loving The Earth and Sea

Can we enjoy these amazingly beautiful islands and be more mindful of our consumption and waste? We say "Yes"! We divert food waste and other organics, before they get to our landfill and use them as valuable sources of inputs for our compost. Win Win!!! Our Mission is to initiate and collaborate on projects that inspire sustainability, especially urban gardening, primarily in, but not limited to, St. Thomas, US Virgin Islands. Our Vision is to be a model in the Caribbean for creative sustainable solutions and to help food and beverage outlets on the island of St. Thomas reduce their compostable waste to landfill by 50% by June, 2025.

Sharing Lifestyle

The Virgin Islands are extremely beautiful, dynamic, and fun place to live and to visit. We love eats, beverages of all sorts, music, festivals, and celebrating. Not to mention our breathtaking views and beaches. We will be highlighting places, people, and things of interest to locals and visitors alike. We understand that you want to make the best use of your time. We can help you get to where you want to go.
