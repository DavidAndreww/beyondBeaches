Loving The Earth and Sea

We rescue valuable food waste from our landfill and use it as a source of inputs for our compost that we use in our organic garden in Frenchman Bay. It is our Mission share with others, collaborate on projects that involve education and practice of sustainable potential and practices. We hope to partner with others to create innovative and simple projects that can grow. We seek to amplify. Please contact us if you would like to get involved or if you are already engaged and would like to collaborate.
