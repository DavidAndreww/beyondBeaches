About Us


Greetings!! Thank you for checking out our site. We ask, is it possible to be a steward of these beautiful islands, while promoting businesses? Which are the local businesses that ask this same question? And which ones answer with a resounding, "YES!"? We leverage technology and local know how to create a network that understands that our islands should be treated as a COMMUNITY where all can thrive and not a COMMODITY, where entrenched patterns of extraction and exploitation create stratification, division, alienation, and isolation. Motivated by a love for the hospitality industry , sustainability, and Caribbean culture, we have built this platform to support local Virgin Island businesses and creatives who embrace togetherness, and inspire sustainable practices, as we share information on current events and happenings of interest for visitors and locals alike.
